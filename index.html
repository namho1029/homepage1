Welcome to My Website

Hello, my name is Namho Kim and I am a second-year student in the Department of Computer Engineering at Kangwon National University.
I have a deep interest in AI, particularly in developing AI technologies for robotics. My goal is to create AI algorithms and models for autonomous robots, co-bots, and human-robot interaction systems. I am focusing on machine learning, deep learning, reinforcement learning, real-time data processing, sensor fusion, and robot vision.
To realize this dream, I have participated in hackathon competition where I used Python and deep learning for image recognition. In the competition, I developed an algorithm to detect whether a safety helmet is worn when using electric scooters. I utilized Convolutional Neural Networks (CNN) to create an accurate and efficient model. This experience has enhanced my practical AI development skills, and I aim to continue improving AI solutions in the future.
